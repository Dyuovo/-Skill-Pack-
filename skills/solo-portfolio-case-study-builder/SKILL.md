# Solo Portfolio Case Study Builder

## Purpose

Transform project artifacts into a credible story that shows product judgment, engineering tradeoffs, execution, and learning.

## Inputs

- PRD, design, release record, incident review, metrics, screenshots, or demo.
- Target audience: hiring manager, client, collaborator, investor, or personal archive.

## Workflow

1. Identify the audience and desired impression.
2. Summarize the problem and user.
3. Show constraints and tradeoffs.
4. Explain the chosen scope and why.
5. Describe implementation at the right depth.
6. Include release/operation proof: version, health, deployment record, metrics, incidents, or before/after.
7. State lessons and next steps.
8. Remove secrets, private data, and sensitive operational details.

## Case Study Template

```markdown
# Case Study: <Project>

## One-Line Summary

## Problem

## User / Context

## Constraints

## Product Decisions

## Technical Decisions

## What Shipped

## Evidence

## Results / Metrics

## What I Learned

## Next Step
```

## Quality Gate

A strong case study shows decisions and evidence, not just features. Include at least one tradeoff and one proof point.

## Persistence

Save to:

```text
docs/portfolio/<project>-case-study.md
```
