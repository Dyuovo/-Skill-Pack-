# Solo Weekly Monthly Review Coach

## Purpose

Keep solo execution honest without creating bureaucracy. Reviews should reduce drift, surface blockers, and turn shipped work into learning.

## Weekly Review Workflow

1. Check project ledger and WIP.
2. List what shipped, what moved, and what got stuck.
3. Review production health for live projects.
4. Decide next week's 1-3 commitments.
5. Move paused or ambiguous work out of active WIP.
6. Record one process improvement.

## Monthly Review Workflow

1. Review shipped outcomes, not just activity.
2. Compare roadmap expectations with reality.
3. Review incidents, near misses, and operational debt.
4. Review growth goals and portfolio artifacts.
5. Re-score project priorities.
6. Decide continue, pause, archive, or double down.

## Blocked

```

## Portfolio Evidence

```

## Quality Gate

End every review with fewer active ambiguities than you started with. If everything remains active, the review failed.
